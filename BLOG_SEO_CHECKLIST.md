## üéØ SEO Best Practices

### Keyword Research
Before writing, think about what your target audience is searching for:
- What questions do they have?
- What problems are they trying to solve?
- What terms would they type into Google?

**Tools to use:**
- Google autocomplete (start typing in Google search)
- Google "People also ask" section
- Google "Related searches" at bottom of results
- Think about your own spiritual journey - what did you search for?

### Content Structure
Make it easy for readers AND Google to understand your content:

```
H1: Main Title (only one per post)
  Introduction paragraph

  H2: First major section
    Content...

    H3: Subsection if needed
      Content...

  H2: Second major section
    Content...

  Conclusion paragraph
```

### Writing for Humans First
- Write naturally, don't force keywords
- Provide genuine value
- Answer questions completely
- Use examples and stories
- Make it engaging and readable

Google's algorithm is smart enough to understand context and meaning. Focus on helping your readers, and SEO will follow.

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Keyword Stuffing
‚ùå **Don't do this:**
> "Meditation is good. Meditation helps you. Learn meditation. Meditation meditation meditation."

‚úÖ **Do this instead:**
> "Meditation is a powerful practice for inner peace. When you meditate regularly, you'll notice reduced stress and increased clarity."

### 2. Duplicate Content
- Don't copy content from other sites
- Don't publish the same content on multiple pages
- Create unique, original content

### 3. Thin Content
- Don't publish posts under 500 words
- Aim for 800-1500 words for most topics
- Provide comprehensive, valuable information

### 4. Ignoring Mobile Users
- Most readers are on mobile
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use plenty of headings to break up text
- Ensure images aren't too large

### 5. No Call-to-Action
- End posts with a question or invitation
- Encourage comments
- Suggest related posts to read
- Ask readers to share if they found it valuable
